Overview

Purpose

My purpose for this website is to provide trips into the great outdoors for adventurous individuals and groups, focusing on white water rafting but also including camping and other outdoor adventures.

Audience

The audience I expect to visit this site are adventurous youth, young adults, and older adults. They will likely be in good financial situations, if they are considering a white-water rafting trip. They may be older adults who have a family with older children, or they are young adults. These groups of people will have their own devices, so it's important that we make the site accessible on multiple types of computers and handheld devices (phones, tablets, etc).
